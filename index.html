About me

Full Stack Developer


Hi!

My name is Annette, and I am a trainee developer with Service NSW. Stories have beginnings, middles and endings, but the start of the story of my Service NSW journey is actually the middle of a larger story that started a number of years ago when I was working in telecommunications. Having worked for Telstra and Optus, the concepts of IT and programming have never been unfamiliar to me, but whilst an amount of conceptual proficiency was easily absorbed by osmosis being immersed in the culture, I never followed the formal path of investigating training opportunities and career development.

So many times have I challenged myself over the years to learn new thing ranging from accounting, to floristry, to jewellery making and resin art, to computer games – and it has come to me that whilst I am good at many endeavours, the one thing I am great at is “learning new skills”.

Fast forward to 2020, and the pandemic of the century has changed the way we live in so many ways. From how we work, to how we travel, to how we watch people lose their way when engaging with Bunnings staff on youtube – it has changed the way society works and in it, we find ourselves challenged to change with it.

I had been with Service NSW for 6 years, beginning in the contact centre and moving to middle office. My previous role in middle office saw me becoming proficient with a number of manual tasks that had had no scope for automation, modernisation and digitisation due to existing prohibitive legislation.

Whilst being an office champion and a heavy contributor to the Circle of Service process review platform, I found myself frustrated by what could and couldn’t be done to improve efficiency not just for my team in the office, but also on the customer side to improve their experience.

Then came COVID 19.

With the work environments changing and the trainee developer secondment opening up, it was an obvious and natural decision for me to take this opportunity when it presented itself. The role itself if perfectly suited to me as it challenges my intuitive and analytical mind and also nurtures my artistic and creative nature. And so here I stand, ready for the next chapter in the story of my Service NSW journey.

My email is annette.brand@service.nsw.gov.au, and I am always open to hearing from anyone who is considering this career progression or on the same path as myself, and I am happy to offer any help or advice that I can.
